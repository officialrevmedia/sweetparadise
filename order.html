How orders work

A few things worth knowing.

01

Cake orders go to Hamilton.

All custom cakes are processed through the Hamilton store. Waterdown carries a selection of cash-and-carry cakes ready to take home.

02

Catering needs lead time.

More notice means more options, especially for weddings, large drop-offs, and full-service events. Give us as much notice as you can.

03

Calls are fastest.

Forms come straight to us, and we reply within one business day. For same-day questions and stock checks, a phone call is the quickest path.
