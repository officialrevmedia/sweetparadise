Home/Pasta Sauce
Homemade Pasta Sauce

A family recipe, on your stove tonight.

Made from plum tomatoes with the recipe our family has been cooking for generations. Slow-simmered, balanced, and ready to ladle. The household staple Hamilton kitchens stock by the jar.

The Sauce

Plum tomatoes, real ingredients, and time on the stove.

The sauce starts where every good Italian sauce starts. Whole plum tomatoes. Good olive oil. Onion, garlic, herbs. Patience. We make it the way it has always been made in our family, and we have not changed the way for one good reason. It works.

Pick up a jar or a tub. Toss it through fresh pasta. Layer it into lasagna. Ladle it over meatballs. Keep one in the fridge and one in the freezer.
