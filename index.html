The Sweet Paradise Experience

More than a bakery. A full Italian food destination.

Step inside and you'll find fresh bread baked that morning, a deli counter stacked with imported cured meats and cheeses, refrigerated cases of scratch-made cakes, a freezer of family-size prepared meals, jars of our homemade pasta sauce, and shelves of Italian pantry staples.

It's where Hamilton families pick up Sunday dinner, where birthdays start, where catering platters leave by the dozen, and where a hundred years of combined baking experience goes into everything we serve.

From Our Family to Yours

Founded by Carmen Mancini.
Carried forward by his family.

In 1986, Carmen Mancini opened a small Italian bakery on Hamilton's mountain with his wife and three sons. Today, Pat, Dino, and Lidio carry that recipe book forward, alongside two locations, a deli counter, a catering operation, and a community that treats us like family because we treat them the same.
