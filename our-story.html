Home  /  Our Story

A family story, baked into Hamilton.

Forty years ago, Carmen Mancini opened a small Italian bakery on Hamilton's mountain. The bread came out hot. The line started forming. It hasn't really stopped.

Carmen Mancini came to Canada with his recipes and his wife.

He opened Sweet Paradise in 1986. Three sons grew up in it. They still run it.

The bakery started small. Bread, pastries, cakes, the kind of Italian baking Carmen had been doing his whole life. The neighbourhood found it fast. Sunday after Sunday, the line at the counter got a little longer. The walk-in cooler got a little fuller. The deli case appeared. The catering jobs started arriving.

Pat, Dino, and Lidio Mancini grew up behind that counter. They learned the recipes from their father. They learned the customers' names. They learned what a wedding looks like when the bread doesn't show up on time, and how to make sure it always does.

Carmen has since passed, but his hands are still in everything we make. The pasta sauce recipe is his. The cake sponges are his. The way we treat customers like family is his.

Today, Sweet Paradise has two locations, a deli, a freezer aisle of prepared meals, a catering operation that handles weddings of five hundred guests, and a Hamilton community that has made us part of birthdays, holidays, baptisms, and Sunday dinners for generations.

When you walk in, we want you to feel like family. Because that's what you are.

Forty Years, In Short

A timeline.

1986

Sweet Paradise opens

Carmen Mancini opens a small Italian bakery on Stone Church Road, Hamilton.

1990s

Deli & catering arrive

The deli counter opens. Catering becomes a major part of the business as Hamilton families and businesses keep calling.

2000s

Pat, Dino & Lidio step in

The next generation takes the lead. Dino's cake program grows into the signature program it is today.

2010s

Waterdown location opens

A second store on Dundas Street E. brings Sweet Paradise to a new community.

Today

A Hamilton staple

Two locations serving bread, cakes, pastries, deli, catering, prepared meals, and Italian grocery. Over 100 years of combined baking experience in the kitchen.
